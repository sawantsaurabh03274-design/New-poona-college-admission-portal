Admission Guidance

Step-by-step assistance through the admission process

Step 1: Application Submission

Complete and submit your online application form. Ensure all personal information is accurate.

  • Create your applicant account
  • Fill in personal and academic details
  • Select your preferred program
  • Review and submit your application
Step 2: Document Submission

Upload all required documents through the portal. Documents can be submitted in PDF or JPEG format.

  • Check the required documents list
  • Scan and upload each document
  • Track submission status
  • Resubmit if documents are rejected
Step 3: Application Review

Your application will be reviewed by the admissions committee. This process may take 2-4 weeks.

  • Monitor your application status
  • Respond promptly to any requests for additional information
  • Check your email regularly for updates
Step 4: Admission Decision

You will receive an admission decision via email and through the portal.

  • Accept your offer by the deadline
  • Submit any required enrollment deposits
  • Complete pre-enrollment requirements
  • Prepare for orientation

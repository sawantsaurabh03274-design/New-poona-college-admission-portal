About Poona College

Established with a vision to provide quality education and shape future leaders

Welcome to Our Prestigious Institution

Poona College has been a cornerstone of educational excellence for decades. We are committed to providing a transformative educational experience that prepares students for success in their chosen fields.

Our campus provides a vibrant learning environment with state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and a diverse student community.

  • NAAC Accredited with 'A+' Grade
  • Well-equipped laboratories and libraries
  • Highly qualified and experienced faculty
  • Excellent placement opportunities
  • Vibrant campus life with various clubs and activities

Admission Process

Follow these simple steps to apply for admission at Poona College

1

Check Eligibility

Review the eligibility criteria for your desired program. Ensure you meet the minimum academic requirements.

  • Minimum percentage requirements
  • Subject-specific prerequisites
  • Entrance exam scores (if applicable)
2

Fill Application Form

Complete the online application form with accurate personal and academic details.

  • Personal information
  • Academic history
  • Program preferences
  • Upload required documents
3

Pay Application Fee

Submit the application fee through our secure online payment gateway.

  • Credit/Debit card
  • Net banking
  • UPI payments
4

Submit & Track

Submit your application and track its status through your student portal.

  • Application number for reference
  • Regular status updates
  • Communication via email/SMS

Admission Requirements

Documents and eligibility criteria needed for the application process

Documents Checklist

  • 10th and 12th Marksheets
  • Transfer Certificate
  • Migration Certificate (if applicable)
  • Character Certificate
  • Category Certificate (if applicable)
  • Passport size photographs
  • Identity proof (Aadhar Card, etc.)

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum 50% marks in 12th standard
  • Subject-specific requirements for certain programs
  • Entrance exam scores for professional courses
  • Valid ID proof for verification
  • Meeting age criteria (if applicable)

Fee Structure

  • Tuition fees vary by program
  • Additional examination fees
  • Library and laboratory charges
  • Hostel fees (if applicable)
  • Scholarships available for eligible students
